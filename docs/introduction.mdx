# Introduction

OneGlanse tracks how your brand appears inside real AI products and turns that data into visibility scores, sentiment signals, competitor co-mentions, and source intelligence — tracked over time.

It captures responses from the real product UIs, not raw model APIs. The difference matters: UI responses include citations, ranked recommendations, and framing that don't appear in direct API calls.

<img src="/images/Mockup-1.png" alt="OneGlanse Dashboard" />

## Your data, your infrastructure

OneGlanse is fully open source and self-hostable. Responses, analytics, and auth sessions live in databases you control — on your local machine or your own VPS. Response analysis calls go directly from your infrastructure to OpenAI or Anthropic. Nothing passes through a third-party server.

Provider auth uses your own accounts. No credentials are stored externally.

## Deployment paths

- **Cloud app** — hosted at [app.oneglanse.com](https://app.oneglanse.com), fastest setup, no installation
- **Local** — full stack on your own machine, interactive provider auth, manual prompt runs
- **Self-hosted** — deploy to your own VPS for recurring scheduling and full data ownership

See [Getting Started](/getting-started) to pick your path.
